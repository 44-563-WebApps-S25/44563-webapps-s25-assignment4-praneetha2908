Macbeth

Act 1, Scene 3 - The Witches Meet Macbeth
FIRST WITCH: Where hast thou been, sister? SECOND WITCH: Killing swine. THIRD WITCH: Sister, where thou? FIRST WITCH: A sailor’s wife had chestnuts in her lap, And munch’d, and munch’d, and munch’d. SECOND WITCH: I’ll give thee a wind. THIRD WITCH: Thou’rt kind. FIRST WITCH: And I another. THIRD WITCH: I myself have all the other, And the very ports they blow. MACBETH: So foul and fair a day I have not seen.
